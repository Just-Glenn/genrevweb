02 — Our Heart

We play for one reason: to lead people closer to Jesus.

Genesis Revelation is a group of five friends who picked up instruments because we wanted people to encounter Jesus in a real way. Every set is arranged to make room for prayer, built to point past the stage.

That's why adoration sits at the center of what we do. We want a room to walk away thinking more about Him than about us.

"They shared their God given talents to elevate everything about the mass. They make me cry, and my heart fills with the beauty of their music. I thank and praise God for that beautiful gift of those students!" - Sally Knorr

Our Mission

Our mission is to build a space where the noise, labels, and expectations of the world fade away, leaving just us and Jesus. We use simple, honest music to welcome youth into Eucharistic Adoration; creating an environment where anyone can step out of the crowd, sit in the quiet, and experience what it truly means to be intimately seen by God, deeply heard by God, and relentlessly loved for exactly who they are.
